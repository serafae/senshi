Makoto Kino (Sailor Jupiter) ♃'s Interests

General

I’m happiest when I’m in the kitchen experimenting with something new — sweets, bento, you name it.


I love tending to my little garden; it reminds me of home and gives me peace when things get chaotic.


Training and martial arts are a big part of my life too — I don’t like to depend on anyone else for protection. But I also enjoy hanging out with my friends and chatting about everyday things.


I guess I’m a bit of a dreamer, always looking for a love story like the one from my past... if it even existed.

Music

I like music that makes me feel something — upbeat pop when I’m cooking, soft love songs when it’s raining outside, and sometimes old ballads that make me nostalgic.


Anything with heart. I’ll always have a soft spot for idol songs and a few classic enka tunes that remind me of my parents.

Movies

I love a good romance flick, especially the kind that makes you tear up a little but leaves you smiling at the end.


Action movies are fun too — I like strong heroines who can take care of themselves. Sometimes I’ll rewatch old martial arts films when I need a little motivation to train harder.

Television

I watch a lot of cooking shows for inspiration, and sometimes I’ll binge dramas when I have a quiet night in.


Usagi keeps trying to get me hooked on all her favorite magical girl shows — like she doesn’t realize I’m already living one!

Books

I read cookbooks like other people read novels.


I’m always on the lookout for new recipes and flavor ideas. I also like romance novels when I’m in the mood — even if they make me blush sometimes. And if it’s about gardening, I’ll read it cover to cover.


There’s something really comforting about learning how to make things grow.

Heroes

My friends — the Sailor Guardians — are my heroes.


Each of them has a strength that inspires me to keep going. Usagi’s kindness, Ami’s wisdom, Rei’s passion, and Minako’s light remind me what real courage looks like. Being a hero isn’t about power — it’s about protecting the people you love.


Someday, I hope I can be that kind of hero too.

About me:

Hey there!


I’m Makoto Kino — though most people at school just call me Mako-chan.


I might look a little intimidating at first, but I promise I’m not as tough as I seem. I love cooking, taking care of plants, and working on new recipes when I’m not training or helping my friends.


I moved out on my own pretty young, so I’ve learned to be independent — but I still dream about finding that special someone who’ll sweep me off my feet someday.


I guess I’m kind of a mix between a tomboy and a romantic, huh?


Life’s all about balance — strength and kindness, heart and muscle.
