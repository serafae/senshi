Ami Mizuno (Sailor Mercury) ☿'s Interests

General

I like calm, peaceful days where I can study by the window or visit the library after school.


I enjoy spending time with my friends, especially when we can talk about our dreams or help each other with homework.


I’m interested in technology and computers, and sometimes I spend time programming or solving puzzles.


I value honesty, intelligence, and kindness — even in small, quiet moments.

Music

I like soft, melodic music that helps me concentrate — piano pieces, classical compositions, or gentle pop songs with meaningful lyrics.


Sometimes I listen to instrumental tracks while studying, because it helps me think clearly.


I also enjoy songs that remind me of friendship or growing up, the kind that make you feel calm and hopeful at the same time.

Movies

I enjoy films that make you think, like mysteries, science fiction, or stories about the human mind and heart.


I also appreciate documentaries about space or the ocean — there’s so much beauty and mystery in both.


Occasionally, my friends invite me to lighter movies, and even if I blush at the sillier ones, I always end up laughing with them.

Television

I don’t watch much TV, but when I do, I like educational programs, science features, or news segments that help me learn something new.


I also enjoy certain dramas that explore the emotions between people, especially those that show quiet strength or compassion.


Sometimes, I’ll watch a comedy my friends recommend just to understand what everyone’s laughing about.

Books

Books are my greatest comfort. I read everything from medical journals to classic novels and poetry.


I love stories that teach me about the human spirit or explore the mysteries of nature and technology.


Even when I’m tired, I’ll stay up just a little longer to finish a chapter — it feels like discovering another world with every page.

Heroes

I admire people who dedicate their lives to helping others — scientists, doctors, teachers, and anyone who uses their knowledge to make the world kinder and safer.


My friends are also my heroes; their courage and warmth inspire me every day.


And, of course, Sailor Moon reminds me that even the smallest kindness can create the biggest change.

About me:


Hello, my name is Ami Mizuno.

I’m a student who deeply enjoys studying, reading, and learning about the world around me.

Many people say I’m quiet or reserved, but I just prefer to think before I speak.

I believe knowledge is one of the greatest strengths we have — whether it’s understanding science, literature, or the emotions that connect people.

I’m also Sailor Mercury, Guardian of Water and Wisdom, and I fight alongside my friends to protect peace and the people we care about.

Who I'd like to meet:
