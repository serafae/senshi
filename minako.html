Minako Aino (Sailor Venus) ♀'s Interests

General

I love everything about love, beauty, and justice, and a little fame never hurts either. I spend a lot of time singing, dancing, and practicing my idol poses in the mirror. Volleyball is serious business, and I’m determined to win the next tournament! I adore shopping with my friends and daydreaming about my next big romance. I also enjoy writing lyrics and love fortunes just for fun, letting my imagination sparkle freely.

Music

Music is one of my greatest passions. I love listening to classical pop idols, catchy upbeat songs that make you want to dance, and anything that can make your heart flutter. I am especially inspired by Three Lights, and someday I hope to release my own hits as Aino Minako. Karaoke with my friends is always a blast, and I never shy away from being the first to perform!

Movies

I can never resist a romantic comedy, especially the cheesier, more over-the-top ones. I also enjoy action movies with brave heroines and anything that features a cute actor or a dramatic story. One of my all-time favorites is The Phantom of the Opera — it is so tragic, so romantic, and so full of emotion that it always leaves me inspired.

Television

I love watching idol contests and talent shows, taking careful notes every time to learn new tricks. Cute anime and magical girl shows brighten my days, and sports matches, especially volleyball, always keep me excited. And of course, I admire anything about Sailor V — she is my hero and the reason I strive to shine every day.

Books

I enjoy reading fashion magazines and idol photobooks to stay inspired and stylish. Romance novels are my favorite, as they make my heart flutter with every story. I also enjoy reading about mythology and goddesses, because a girl must understand her legacy. Of course, I keep my own secret adventure logs as Sailor V, which I treasure dearly.

Heroes

Sailor Moon is my shining leader and my dearest friend, and she inspires me to fight for what is right every single day. As Sailor Venus — the original guardian of love and justice — I strive to be a hero in my own way, spreading hope, courage, and a little glamour wherever I go. My fellow Sailor Guardians are unstoppable together, and I couldn’t do it without them! Artemis, my loyal (and occasionally bossy) partner, keeps me on track and reminds me to always give my best. I’m also inspired by idols and performers who chase their dreams, as well as anyone who believes in love and never gives up — those are the heroes I admire most.
